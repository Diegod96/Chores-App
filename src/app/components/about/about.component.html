How's It Work?

Once you've added your children's accounts you'll be able to log in here, add a chores list and decide on rewards your children will be able to earn. Your child/ren will be able to log into their accounts, view the chores and send you a 'completion' request when they have done their chore. Once that's pending, you can review if the chore has been done and approve it which will award them points.

If your child is late on completing their chore you will have the option to extend their deadling or deduct points from their account.

Once they have earned enough points, your child/ren will be able to redeem them for the reward you've set!
